## Dyslexia Statistics

International Dyslexia Association. (n.d.). *Dyslexia basics*. Retrieved from https://dyslexiaida.org/dyslexia-basics/

> "As many as 15–20% of the population has a language-based learning disability. Dyslexia is the most common of the language-based learning disabilities." (IDA, dyslexia-basics)

## Accessibility Standards

World Wide Web Consortium. (2023). *Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2*. https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/

Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1 for normal text (Level AA). Lume targets AA compliance for all text including emphasized and overlaid text.

## Hyphenation Algorithm

Knuth, D. E., & Plass, M. F. (1981). Breaking paragraphs into lines. *Software: Practice and Experience*, 11(11), 1119–1184.
